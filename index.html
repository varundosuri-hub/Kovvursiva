Our Story

A family kitchen,
two decades of feasts.

Sri Varalakshmi Catering was born in Rajahmundry in the early 2000s, when our founder began cooking banana-leaf meals for neighbourhood weddings. What began as a family pledge — only pure vegetarian, only fresh — grew into Coastal Andhra’s most-loved catering house.

Every dish still passes through the hands of our head chef. Spices are pounded in-house. Ghee is home-rendered. Rice is sourced from Godavari delta paddy. Nothing reaches your guests that we wouldn’t proudly serve our own family.
